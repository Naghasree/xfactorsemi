Who We Are

XFactor Semiconductor is a specialized consulting firm with deep expertise in the semiconductor packaging industry. We bridge the gap between technology and investment, helping both investors and entrepreneurs make informed, strategic decisions.

Our comprehensive background and contractual agreements enable us to work with foundries, Outsourced Assembly and Test (OSAT) providers, Equipment Manufacturers, Materials Suppliers, Component Suppliers, and Integrated (IDM) or Fabless Chip Design Companies.

Our Mission

To make decision-making a competitive advantage for our clients by sharing our background in semiconductor technology and continuous research insights.

Our Vision

To be the leading semiconductor advisory firm, guiding investors towards returns and enabling entrepreneurs to bring breakthrough technologies to market.
